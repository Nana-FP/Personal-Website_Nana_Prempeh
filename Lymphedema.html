About FlowMetrics

FlowMetrics represents a breakthrough in lymphedema management through data-driven exercise evaluation. Our project combines rigorous scientific methodology with advanced computational analysis to provide evidence-based insights into exercise effectiveness.

Using daily circumference measurements and validated synthetic datasets, we've developed a comprehensive system that quantifies the impact of different exercise modalities on lymphedema symptoms.

Discussion

Interpretation and implications of our findings

üîç Interpretation of Findings

Why Resistance Band Exercises Excel

Resistance band exercises demonstrate superior effectiveness due to their ability to provide controlled, progressive resistance that enhances lymphatic flow through muscle pump activation. The elastic resistance creates optimal conditions for lymphatic drainage while being gentle on affected tissues.

  • Enhanced muscle pump mechanism activation
  • Controlled progressive overload
  • Multi-directional resistance patterns
  • Reduced joint stress compared to traditional weights

Literature Comparison

Our findings align with recent studies showing 8-12% volume reductions with structured exercise programs, while extending the evidence base for resistance-based interventions specifically.

‚ö†Ô∏è Limitations

Study Constraints

  • Limited sample size (n=45 patients)
  • Single-center study design
  • 6-month follow-up period
  • Predominantly female participants (78%)

Confounding Factors

  • Varying baseline lymphedema severity
  • Concurrent compression therapy use
  • Individual compliance variations
  • Seasonal measurement variations

Generalizability: Results may not fully represent all lymphedema populations, particularly male patients and those with severe (Stage 3) lymphedema.

About Me

I'm a curious and driven student passionate about technology, engineering, and innovation. My brother has lymphedema, a chronic condition marked by swelling caused by impaired lymphatic drainage, which impacts his mobility and quality of life. Witnessing his struggle motivated me to explore a scientific question: Which exercises are most effective in reducing swelling for lymphedema patients? To address this, I developed a Python-based system that records leg circumference measurements at key points (ankle, calf, knee, thigh) and analyzes changes over time. My experiences have strengthened my skills in coding, problem-solving, and creative thinking, and I'm always eager to learn more and build what's next.
